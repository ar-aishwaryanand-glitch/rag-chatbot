COMPANY REMOTE WORK POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Overview:
This document outlines the remote work policy for all employees. Our company supports flexible work arrangements to promote work-life balance and productivity.

Eligibility:
All full-time employees who have completed their probationary period (90 days) are eligible for remote work arrangements. Part-time employees may request remote work on a case-by-case basis.

Remote Work Options:
1. Fully Remote: Employees work from home 5 days per week
2. Hybrid: Employees split time between office and home (minimum 2 days in office)
3. Flexible: Employees can choose their work location daily

Equipment and Technology:
- Company will provide a laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse
- Employees are responsible for providing a suitable workspace
- High-speed internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps) is required
- VPN access is mandatory for all remote connections
- Technical support is available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST

Communication Expectations:
- Daily check-ins with team via Slack or Microsoft Teams
- Video cameras must be on during team meetings
- Response time to emails: within 4 business hours
- Response time to urgent Slack messages: within 1 hour
- Core collaboration hours: 10 AM - 3 PM EST

Security Requirements:
- Use company-provided VPN for all work activities
- Do not access company systems from public Wi-Fi without VPN
- Keep all software and security patches up to date
- Report any security incidents immediately to IT
- Lock computer when stepping away from desk

Performance Expectations:
Remote employees are expected to maintain the same productivity levels as in-office employees. Performance will be measured based on:
- Completion of assigned tasks and projects
- Meeting deadlines
- Quality of work
- Communication and collaboration with team

Home Office Stipend:
Employees working remotely 3+ days per week are eligible for a $500 annual stipend for home office equipment and supplies.

Questions:
For questions about this policy, contact HR at hr@company.com or your direct manager.
